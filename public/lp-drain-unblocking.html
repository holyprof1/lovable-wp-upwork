Frequently Asked Questions

What causes a blocked drain?
The most common causes are accumulated grease and food waste in kitchen drains, hair and soap in bathroom drains, and foreign objects in toilets. Tree root ingress is common in older external drains.
Can I unblock it myself?
For minor blockages, drain unblocker or a plunger sometimes works. For anything beyond a surface blockage, professional tools are needed and DIY attempts can push the blockage deeper.
