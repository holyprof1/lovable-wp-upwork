Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I have a hidden leak?+

Common signs include an unexplained rise in water bills, damp patches on walls or ceilings, mould appearing in unexpected places, the sound of running water when everything is off, or soft spots in flooring.

How do you find a hidden leak without opening everything up?+

Afonso uses thermal imaging and a compact inspection camera to accurately locate leaks before any access is opened.

Can you repair the leak in the same visit?+

In most cases yes. If the repair requires specialist parts or significant access, Afonso will be upfront about the timeline.

How much damage can a slow leak cause?+

Even a pinhole leak running over weeks can cause thousands of pounds of damage to timber, plaster, and in worst cases electrical systems. Finding it quickly is the most cost-effective outcome.

Will you need to open my walls or floors?+

Afonso's minimal disruption policy means we only open the minimum access required once the location is confirmed. We don't start pulling up floors to look.

Do you offer a warranty?+

Yes. 12 months on all completed repair work.

Do you charge a call-out fee?+

No. You pay for the investigation and agreed repair work.
